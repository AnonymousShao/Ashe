The UOS deployment system becomes simpler.

UOS Director

Deployment

Can quickly deploy the entire cluster

Deterministic

Arranging templates to makeclusters highly accurate

Controllable

Flexible custom deployment architecture

Management

The deployment is delivered in a number of ways

Reuse

Do not repeat the wheel, UOS Director take full advantage of OpenStack existing services to complete UOS platform deployment.

Efficient

In the deterministic hardware and network topology, can achieve a key deployment of rapid deployment of UOS platform.

Flexible

Customized by node roles, you can customize a variety of deployment architectures and support service expansion, perfect integration of external systems.

Solve the following pain points

Integrate TripleO to improve delivery efficiency

Due to the huge and complex system of OpenStack, its delivery has long been a difficult problem to solve. UOS Director making the deployment of a high degree of certainty, improve deployment efficiency.

Role customization, flexible custom deployment architecture

UOS Director through the role of the role can be flexible to customize the cloud environment will be deployed, specify which roles contain what services, More flexible customization of your environment.

Network customization, flexible custom network topology

The most complex of OpenStack is the network architecture. UOS Director takes advantage of the networking capabilities provided by Neutron to abstract the cluster into multiple networks.

Bare machine management, centralized management of cluster information

UOS Director enables bare metal management capabilities through the community Ironic project, manages the entire lifecycle of physical servers in the cluster, and automatically collects server parameters, topology.
